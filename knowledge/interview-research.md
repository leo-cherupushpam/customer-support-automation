### Confidence Threshold Analysis

**Industry Insights:**
- 75% of consumers are in favor of agents using AI to draft responses
- 68% of consumers trust AI agents with human-like traits
- 85% say transitions from AI to human are seamless

**Your Decision:**
- **85% confidence threshold** aligns with industry expectations
- Below 85% → escalate to human (maintains quality)
- Above 85% → auto-respond (achieves deflection goal)

## Common Pain Points (From Research)

### Top 5 Support Manager Pain Points

1. **Ticket Volume Explosion**
   - 57% expect call volumes to increase 20% in 1-2 years
   - Average: 250-350 tickets/week per manager

2. **Administrative Overhead**
   - 18% of time wasted on admin tasks (7+ hours/week)
   - 37% of technicians say admin keeps them from actual work

3. **Lack of Context**
   - 26% of reps say they lack customer situation context
   - 80% believe better data access would improve work

4. **AI Skepticism**
   - Biggest fear: AI embarrassing the brand
   - Concern: Quality vs. automation tradeoff

5. **Integration Fragmentation**
   - Already using multiple AI tools
   - Problem: Tools don't work together

## Interview Question Refinements

### Original → Refined with Research Context

**Question 1: Ticket Volume**
- *Original*: "How many tickets do you handle per week?"
- *Refined*: "With 57% of teams expecting 20% volume increase in 1-2 years, what's your current weekly volume and projected growth?"

**Question 2: Current Deflection**
- *Original*: "What's your current deflection rate?"
- *Refined*: "With industry at 30% AI resolution, what's your current deflection rate and what would 'success' look like for you?"

**Question 3: AI Concerns**
- *Original*: "What concerns do you have about AI automation?"
- *Refined*: "Given that 75% support consumers favor AI drafting responses but fear brand embarrassment, what's your biggest concern about AI automation?"

**Question 4: Success Metrics**
- *Original*: "How do you measure CSAT?"
- *Refined*: "With 90% of CX leaders reporting positive ROI on AI, what metrics matter most: deflection rate, CSAT, resolution time, or cost per ticket?"

**Question 5: Integration Needs**
- *Original*: "What integrations would you need?"
- *Refined*: "Given fragmentation concerns, which integrations are non-negotiable: Zendesk, Intercom, Slack, or something else?"

## Industry Terminology Glossary

| Term | Definition | Why It Matters |
|------|------------|----------------|
| **Deflection Rate** | % of tickets resolved without human | Primary success metric |
| **CSAT** | Customer Satisfaction Score (1-5) | Quality measure |
| **Tier-1 Tickets** | Simple, repetitive questions (FAQ, password reset) | Target for automation |
| **Tier-2/3 Tickets** | Complex issues requiring human expertise | Escalation target |
| **AI Copilot** | AI that assists humans (not replaces) | Human-in-the-loop approach |
| **CX Trendsetter** | Early AI adopters | Your ideal beta users |
| **CX Traditionalist** | Late AI adopters | Future market |
