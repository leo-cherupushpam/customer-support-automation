## LinkedIn DM Templates

### Initial Outreach Message
```
Hi [Name],

I'm building an AI customer support automation tool and looking to interview 10 support managers about their pain points and requirements.

Would you be open to a 30-minute chat next week? Happy to share findings with you.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```

### Follow-up Message (3 days later)
```
Hi [Name],

Just following up on my message about interviewing support managers for my AI customer support project.

I know you're busy, but I'd really value your perspective. The chat would only take 30 minutes.

Let me know if you're interested!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### Confirmation Message (after booking)
```
Thanks for booking, [Name]!

Looking forward to our chat on [Date] at [Time].

Link: [Calendly/Zoom Link]

Questions I'll cover:
- Your current support workflow
- Biggest pain points
- AI automation concerns
- Success metrics you track

See you then!
[Your Name]
```
