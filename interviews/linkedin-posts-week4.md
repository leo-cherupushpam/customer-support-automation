## Post 3: Week 4 Wrap-up

**Title**: Week 4 wrap-up - 50+ tickets tested, here's what we learned

**Post**:
```
Week 4 complete! Here's what happened:

✅ 5 support teams recruited for beta
✅ 50+ real tickets processed
✅ Deflection rate: 52% (target: 60%)
✅ CSAT: 4.2/5 (target: 4.0)
✅ Average resolution time: 1.5 min (target: <2 min)

Key learnings:
1. Template responses work well for simple tickets (password reset, billing)
2. Feature requests always need human review (makes sense!)
3. Urgency detection is critical for priority assignment
4. Confidence threshold of 85% is about right

Next steps:
• Improve response templates based on feedback
• Add more category support
• Implement LLM-based responses for personalization

Full case study coming in Week 6!

#CustomerSupport #AI #BuildInPublic #ProductManagement #DataDriven
```
