# AI Customer Support Automation Platform

**Build-in-Public Journey**: 6-week journey to build a multi-agent AI support system that deflects 60% of tickets while maintaining 4.0+ CSAT.

## One-Line PM Pitch
Support teams handle 1000+ tickets/week manually. I built an AI deflection system, measured deflection rate + CSAT, and documented the B2B sales motion.

## Problem
Customer support scales linearly with users but costs shouldn't. Support managers drown in repetitive tickets that could be automated.

## Hypothesis
If AI can auto-resolve 60% of tier-1 tickets without reducing CSAT, support teams will reduce costs by 30-40%.

## Success Metrics
- **Deflection Rate**: ≥60% of tickets resolved without human
- **CSAT Score**: ≥4.0/5 customer satisfaction
- **Resolution Time**: <2 minutes average
- **Escalation Rate**: ≤40% of tickets requiring human handoff

## Architecture
4-agent team:
1. **Triage Agent**: Classify tickets + confidence scoring
2. **Response Agent**: AI draft responses with KB lookup
3. **Escalation Agent**: Human handoff when confidence <85%
4. **Learning Agent**: Learn from resolved tickets, update KB
